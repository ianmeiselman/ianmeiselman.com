Ian Meiselman

Finance Student — University of South Florida
Focus: Commercial Real Estate

Background

Ian Meiselman is a Finance student at the University of South Florida with a focus on commercial real estate. Raised in Florida, early exposure to property ownership and hands-on work around construction and operations sparked a long-term interest in real estate as both a business and a craft.

Experience

Ian began gaining practical experience at a young age, purchasing his first property at 19 and managing it independently. That experience shaped his focus on acquisitions and the process of identifying, evaluating, and securing opportunities. He has continued to build a foundation in real estate through academic work and hands-on analysis, with an emphasis on understanding pricing, risk, and long-term value.

Leadership

Ian currently serves as Vice President of the University of South Florida Real Estate Society, where he helps coordinate events and connect students with professionals in the industry. His involvement reflects a continued focus on evaluating opportunities and developing a disciplined approach to investment decision-making.

Personal

Outside of real estate, Ian spends time in physically demanding and outdoor environments, including kiteboarding and spearfishing. These activities have shaped his discipline, risk awareness, and ability to stay composed in dynamic conditions. He is also focused on maintaining a high standard of personal health and performance, which carries over into his academic and professional work.
